# Memorising C Operators

## Arithmetic Operators
- \+ add
- \- subtract
- \* multiply
- / divide
- % modulus
- ++ increment
- -- decrement

## Relational Operators
- == equal
- != not equal
- \> greater than
- < less than
- \>= greater than or equal to
- <= less than or equal to

## Logical Operators
- && and
- || or
- ! not
- ? : ternary

## Bitwise Operators
- & and
- | or
- ^ xor
- ~ one's complement
- << shift left
- \>> shift right

## Assignment Operators
- = equal
- += plus-equal
- -= minus-equal
- *= multiply-equal
- /= divide-equal
- %= modulus-equal
- <<= shift left-equal
- \>>= shift right-equal
- &= and-equal
- ^= xor-equal
- |= or-equal

## Data Operators
- sizeof() get the size of
- [] array subscript
- & address of
- \* the value of
- -> structure dereference
- . structure reference
